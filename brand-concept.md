# Smart City — brand concept (Smart Zone-inspired command center)

## Name + tagline
SMART CITY — הילדים שמתקנים את העיר עם AI. Hebrew RTL hackathon platform (Sderot, Israel).

## Audience + tone
Kids 10-16 at municipal hackathons + instructors. Tone: mission-control operator — kids feel like they run a city command center. Serious sci-fi, not childish.

## Visual DNA (from Behance "Visualization for Smart Zone", gallery 71523959)
1. **Deep space base**: near-black navy #020509 → #0A1424, radial vignette, faint particle field
2. **Holographic city**: wireframe building silhouettes in glowing cyan #35E1FF with node dots at corners; scan-line shimmer
3. **Two-accent system**: CYAN (#35E1FF / #0FB8D8) = data/coverage/system; AMBER-GOLD (#FFB627 / #E89B0C) = hazards/selection/roads. Gold hatched (diagonal stripes) fills for highlighted zones
4. **Data cards**: translucent panels rgba(6,16,28,.72), 1px cyan border at 35% opacity, sharp corners with 8px corner tick marks (all 4 corners), tiny square bullet before titles
5. **Hero numbers**: Space Grotesk 700, 28-44px, inside thin rectangle frame with unit suffix small; glow text-shadow
6. **Titles**: centered, letterspaced, flanked by thin bracket ornaments ─┤ TITLE ├─
7. **Leader lines**: 1px lines with end dots connecting labels to map points; dotted/dashed borders everywhere
8. **Charts/bars**: thin outlined, cyan→gold gradient fills

## Typography
- Display/numbers: Space Grotesk (500/700), wide tracking
- Hebrew UI: Rubik (400-900)

## Anti-patterns
- NO rounded blobby cards (max 4px radius; prefer sharp + corner ticks)
- NO purple/pink candy gradients
- NO generic bootstrap buttons; buttons = outlined HUD chips with glow hover
- NO flat gray panels — always translucent navy + border glow

## Motion
Scan sweeps, blinking beacon dots, pulse rings on pins, number count-up, panel border shimmer on hover. Subtle, 60fps, CSS only.
